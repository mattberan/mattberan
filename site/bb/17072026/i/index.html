The Take

Are we experiencing entropy as a result of a natural progression, or is it being introduced on purpose?

By now you know that billionaires run the world right?

Larry Page, Sergey Brin and Michael Dell have more power to change our systems than many of the people in charge of those systems.

So, assuming this is accurate; are things really bad right now by choice? Or by coincidence?

This is the question that keeps spinning around in my head and I can't seem to kick it. I want to know who is profiting off of this chaos, who is benefiting from all this pain and fear?

Because once we find the money, I think we'll find the culprits. Who is getting richer right now? Whose life is improving? Is it just and balanced?
