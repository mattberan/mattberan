Worth Your Time

One of the biggest challenges to leveraging AI in Service Management and shared service functions is measurement; and seeing how my team is pitching this has me feeling better AND worse.

As a software vendor, we build features our customers want. It's kind of our secret sauce: implement with customers so we learn TOGETHER, listen to them for features so we can build better features TOGETHER... you get the idea.

And as such, our customers are asking for ways to measure:
1 - AI effectiveness; how many 'tickets' does it handle autonomously (if at all)?
2 - AI Adoption; how many agents are using AI features and to what level?
3 - What knowledge and automation gaps need filling the most?

Everything you need to know. Nothing you don't.
