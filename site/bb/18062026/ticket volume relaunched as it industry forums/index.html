Something Cool

I'm launching a new 'set' of communities; it's going to be organized by sector/vertical and connect the people in our industry that are solving similar problems with similar constraints.

After years of trying to convince associations to start a peer forum for people in each vertical/industry/sector to connect and learn from each other's successes and mistakes - - I'm going to start it because I'm tired of waiting.

Full disclosure that InvGate is going to be running this - but it will be public (as-in you don't need to be a customer, or even like InvGate - they are just hosting the infrastructure and connecting us to people and other resources).

To see a preview of where we are heading for panels: https://invgate.com/industry-forums/public-sector - this is the one I hosted earlier this week.

It felt like Ticket Volume again - so I'm so excited!

With plans to add roundtables, online forums and meetups - let's unlock the value of our PEOPLE!

For now, it's just getting started and I truly hope it helps connect people ACROSS networks!

If you'd like to be a tent pole in your industry - reach out, I'd love to have you lead the way for your career!
